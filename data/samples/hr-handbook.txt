AtlasRAG Corp — Employee Handbook (HR Policy)
==============================================
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Version: 3.2

1. Vacation and Time Off
All full-time employees receive 20 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation days accrue monthly at a rate of 1.67 days per month. Unused vacation may be carried over to the next calendar year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Employees must submit vacation requests at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal.

2. Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. A formal remote work agreement must be signed before starting remote work. The company provides a one-time stipend of $500 for home office equipment. Remote workers must be available during core hours of 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local time zone.

3. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted semi-annually in June and December. Each review includes a self-assessment, peer feedback, and manager evaluation. Ratings are on a 1-5 scale: 1 (Needs Improvement), 2 (Developing), 3 (Meets Expectations), 4 (Exceeds Expectations), 5 (Outstanding). Employees rated 4 or above are eligible for a merit increase of 3-8%.

4. Benefits Overview
Health insurance is provided through BlueCross BlueShield with three plan options: Basic, Standard, and Premium. The company covers 80% of the premium for the Standard plan. Dental and vision coverage is included at no additional cost. A 401(k) retirement plan is available with a 4% company match after one year of employment.

5. Leave of Absence
Employees may request unpaid leave of absence for up to 12 weeks for qualifying reasons under FMLA. Maternity and paternity leave provides 16 weeks of fully paid leave. Bereavement leave of 5 days is provided for immediate family members. Jury duty leave is fully paid for the duration of service.

6. Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior and treat colleagues with respect. Discrimination, harassment, and retaliation are strictly prohibited. Violations should be reported to HR or through the anonymous ethics hotline at 1-800-555-ETHIC. All reports are investigated within 10 business days.
